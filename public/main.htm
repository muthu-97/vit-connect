Features

  • Calendar

    Ever missed out on the happening events on campus? Calendar is a two-way street giving Clubs, Departments & Regional Associations a channel to publicize their upcoming events and ensuring you don't miss out on another minute of action on campus.

  • Problem Redressal System (P.R.S)

    Have you ever felt that your concerns have fallen on deaf ears? P.R.S provides you with the perfect opportunity to connect with your Student Representatives helping you voice your concerns and seek solutions in an efficient manner.

  • VIT Trade

    Ever faced a problem buying a trunk or selling your books? VIT Trade is your one stop destination for Buying and Selling.

  • Travel Planner

    Looking for company on your next trip to Chennai or Bangalore? Look no further, Travel Planner will put you in touch with fellow VITians to make your journey easier.
